Who am I?

I am a software engineer with a passion for anything gaming. I started out programming in middle school, creating a little game using Scratch with my friends. I was hooked. Ever since then, I carried that passion with me, learning new languages and skills while trying new things with an eager and open mind. Using my experiences in public entertainment, managerial positions, and even educational institutions, I am confident in my ability to pick things up quickly in a very fast paced environment, and hit the ground running with any company and any team. When I'm not writing code, you'll often find me playing and test-running games, reading novels, and visiting local restaurants.
