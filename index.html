    Pollidify is an application that aims to poll participants at a world wide scale, and allow users to express their polls. Created in a week, we decided to implement a project that aims to increase the amount of free data to be used by surveyors and researchers worldwide.

    What’s in the Fridge? helps users find recipes based upon ingredients they already have in their fridge! At first, we looked at other recipe sites to gather some ideas, but eventually we decided to create our own unique twist on getting your next meal plan ready!
